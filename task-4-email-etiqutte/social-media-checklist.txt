Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
? Do’s
* Think before posting – ensure your content is appropriate and respectful. 
* Use strong privacy settings to control who can view your posts and personal information. 
* Verify information before sharing to prevent spreading fake news or misinformation. 
* Maintain a professional digital image, especially on platforms like LinkedIn. 
* Engage positively and respectfully in discussions and comments. 
? Don’ts
* Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords). 
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content that may harm others. 
* Don’t accept requests from unknown or suspicious accounts. 
* Don’t overshare your location or daily routine, which can risk your safety. 
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.
