Stay Safe Online

1.	Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank. 
2.	Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers or online buyers/sellers. QR codes are usually used to pay, not to receive money. 
3.	Check UPI collect requests carefully before approving them in apps like Google Pay, Paytm, or PhonePe. 
4.	Enable app lock and two-factor authentication on your payment and email apps. 
5.	Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email claiming to offer rewards or urgent account updates. 
6.	Install apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. 
7.	Use secure Wi-Fi networks and avoid making financial transactions on public Wi-Fi. 
8.	Regularly monitor your bank account and transaction history for suspicious activity. 
9.	Update your phone and apps regularly to fix security vulnerabilities. 
10.	Report cyber fraud immediately through the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Cyber Crime Helpline – 1930.
